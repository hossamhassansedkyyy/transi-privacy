Delete Your Transi Account

You can request the deletion of your Transi account and all associated personal data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to hossamhassansedkyyy@gmail.com with:

  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • The phone number associated with your account
  • Your full name

What Data Is Deleted

The following data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days of your request:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Your password hash
  • Your device identifier
  • Your booking history and tickets
  • Push notification tokens

Data We May Retain

We may retain certain transaction records for up to 12 months for accounting and legal compliance purposes. These records do not contain personally identifiable information.
